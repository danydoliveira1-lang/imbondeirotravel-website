Experience the wild beauty of Angola on an unforgettable adventure to Kissama National Park, the country's premier wildlife reserve. Located just south of Luanda, this full-day safari offers the perfect opportunity to discover Angola's diverse landscapes, unique wildlife, and breathtaking natural scenery.

Travel through vast savannahs, acacia woodlands, and scenic river plains as you embark on a guided game drive in search of some of Africa's most iconic animals. Keep your camera ready for sightings of elephants, giraffes, zebras, antelopes, ostriches, and a variety of bird species that call the park home.

Visitors may also have the chance to spot the famous Palanca Negra Gigante (Giant Sable Antelope), Angola's national symbol and one of Africa's rarest antelopes. Enjoy the tranquility of the African wilderness, learn about local conservation efforts, and take in spectacular landscapes far from the bustle of the city.

Read Details Tour

Experience one of Africa's most spectacular natural wonders on an exclusive journey to Kalandula Falls, a breathtaking waterfall nestled in the heart of Malanje Province. Combining nature, culture, history, and refined comfort, this carefully curated escape offers an unforgettable introduction to Angola's remarkable landscapes.

Day 1 - Journey to Kalandula Falls

Depart Luanda in the comfort of a private premium vehicle and travel through the scenic landscapes of northern Angola. Upon arrival in Malanje Province, enjoy a guided visit to the magnificent Kalandula Falls, one of the largest waterfalls in Africa, where millions of liters of water cascade over a dramatic horseshoe-shaped cliff surrounded by lush tropical vegetation.

Take in spectacular viewpoints, capture stunning photographs, and experience the soothing sounds of nature from exclusive vantage points. After a gourmet lunch, continue to your selected accommodation for relaxation and an elegant evening immersed in the tranquility of the Angolan countryside.

Day 2 - Pedras Negras de Pungo Andongo & Return to Luanda

Following breakfast, travel to the legendary Pedras Negras de Pungo Andongo, a remarkable collection of giant black rock formations steeped in Angolan history and folklore. Explore the site with your expert guide while learning about the Kingdom of Ndongo and the fascinating story of Queen Nzinga.

Enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding plains before a leisurely lunch and a comfortable return journey to Luanda.

Sustainability

Responsible Travel With Purpose

Travel should create positive and lasting benefits for both visitors and the communities they encounter.

At Imbondeiro Travel, we believe that travel should create positive and lasting benefits for both visitors and the communities they encounter. Our commitment to responsible tourism is reflected in the experiences we design, the partners we work with, and the destinations we promote.

We strive to showcase Angola's extraordinary cultural heritage, natural landscapes, and local traditions while encouraging meaningful and respectful interactions between travelers and host communities. By supporting local businesses, guides, artisans, and service providers, we contribute to the sustainable growth of tourism and the economic well-being of the destinations we serve.

Our Commitment

  • Supporting local communities through responsible tourism practices
  • Promoting and preserving Angola's cultural heritage and traditions
  • Encouraging respectful engagement with local people and customs
  • Partnering with locally owned businesses and suppliers whenever possible
  • Promoting environmental awareness and responsible travel behavior
  • Encouraging the protection of wildlife, natural habitats, and biodiversity
  • Creating authentic experiences that generate positive social and economic impact

Travel That Makes a Difference

We encourage our guests to travel thoughtfully, respect local cultures, minimize their environmental footprint, and contribute positively to the places they visit. Through responsible tourism, we aim to help preserve Angola's unique heritage and natural beauty for future generations while creating enriching experiences for today's travelers.

Our Vision

To promote sustainable and responsible tourism that celebrates cultural diversity, supports local communities, protects natural environments, and inspires meaningful connections between people and destinations across Angola and the world.

Together, we can create memorable journeys that leave a positive impact long after the trip has ended.
